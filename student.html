Looking for Students

We are always looking for highly motivated and energetic students to research topics related to intelligent underwater acoustic and sea-space wireless communications, intelligent signal processing, and corresponding engineering development. Candidates are expected to have solid background in signal processing, wireless communications, and/or artificial intelligence. Passion, collaboration, hard work, and strong motivation towards academic excellence should be necessary, while strong mathematical background and/or hands-on system implementation is a big plus. If interested and have the right background, please email us at yuzhouli@hust.edu.cn.
